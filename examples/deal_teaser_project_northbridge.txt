Opportunity name: Project Northbridge
Transaction type: GP-led continuation vehicle
Seller type: Existing LPs in Alder Ridge Capital Fund IV, with the sponsor leading a continuation process
Manager / sponsor: Alder Ridge Capital, a 2016-vintage mid-market buyout manager focused on vertical software and business services
Existing fund: Alder Ridge Capital Fund IV
Portfolio / asset: ComplianceCloud, a provider of regulatory workflow software for insurance brokers and carriers in the US and UK

What is being sold:
- A new continuation vehicle would acquire 92% of ComplianceCloud from Fund IV.
- Existing LPs can elect to sell for cash or roll into the new vehicle.
- The lead buyer is being asked to anchor the transaction with an approximate $180M equity check, with the remainder syndicated to existing and new investors.

Why seller is transacting:
- Fund IV is beyond its original hold period.
- The sponsor believes the asset still has additional runway and wants more time to execute the next phase of value creation.
- The process is described as primarily a liquidity solution for existing LPs rather than a distressed sale.

Use of proceeds:
- Provide liquidity to selling LPs.
- Establish a modest reserve for tuck-in M&A and product expansion.

Valuation framing:
- Deal priced at 15.5x LTM EBITDA.
- Sponsor argues public software peers and recent private transactions support 16.5x-18.0x for similar assets.
- No third-party valuation materials are included in the teaser.

Company financials:
- FY2025 revenue: $78M
- FY2025 EBITDA: $26M
- Revenue CAGR since 2022: 17%
- Recurring revenue: 84%
- Gross revenue retention: 95%
- Net revenue retention: 111%
- The teaser describes strong cash conversion but does not provide a formal QoE.

Business quality and growth plan:
- ComplianceCloud provides regulatory workflow software used by insurance brokers and carriers.
- The product is positioned as mission-critical because customers rely on it for compliance-heavy operating workflows.
- Growth plan centers on expanding carrier-side modules, increasing wallet share in existing broker accounts, and completing 1-2 tuck-in acquisitions over the next 24 months.

Concentration and exposure:
- Single-asset transaction.
- Top 10 customers represent 28% of revenue.
- Largest customer represents 6% of revenue.
- Geography split is roughly 72% US and 28% UK.

GP alignment and track record:
- Sponsor rolling 100% of carry and approximately 35% of gross proceeds into the continuation vehicle.
- Prior Alder Ridge funds reported net multiples of 1.8x, 2.2x, and 1.9x.
- Fund IV is currently marked at 1.6x gross MOIC.

Current diligence gaps:
- No third-party quality of earnings report in the teaser.
- No detailed churn cohorts by customer size.
- No product diligence materials yet.
- Limited information on the acquisition pipeline.

Remaining unfunded commitments:
- None, other than a proposed reserve inside the continuation vehicle.
